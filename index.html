Discover Our Location

Nyawa Inn & Resort is situated in Tongo, a diamond mining town and the second-largest city in the Kenema District, Eastern Province of Sierra Leone. Tongo is approximately twenty-seven miles away from Kenema, the city covers an area of 60 square miles with a population of 44,376 residents and is one of the largest diamond-producing regions in Sierra Leone.

Tongo is known for its rich cultural diversity, with a mix of various ethnic and religious groups coexisting in the city. This diversity is largely attributed to the thriving diamond mining industry in the area, which draws people from all corners of Sierra Leone. Nyawa Inn & Resort stands as a notable tourist attraction in Tongo, offering a unique experience for visitors.
